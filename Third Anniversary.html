Third Anniversary

29 June 2023 (15:00 PM - 16:00 PM)
Nile Hotel

ArabDT is proud to celebrate its 4th anniversary with a special event on Mar 22, 2023 at The Nile hotel. The event will be a night of celebration, networking, and inspiration, as we look back on our accomplishments and look forward to the future. The evening will begin with a cocktail reception, followed by a sit-down dinner. There will be live music, dancing, and a special presentation about ArabDT's history and future plans. We will also be honoring our employees and partners who have made ArabDT a success.
